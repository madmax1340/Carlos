Budget PC Build Early 2019

(Prices may vary on location and time)

  • CPU: Ryzen 5 2600

    • This processor has 6 cores and a clock speed of 3.4ghz which can easily tackle most programs or games. This processor is a great budget cpu and a low price with great performance. $165

  • Motherboard: Gigabyte b450m Auros M

    • This motherboard is nothing too special but for the price does everything it needs to. It even has RGB headers for RGB lights and fans. $75

  • Ram: G.Skill Aegis 16gb (2x8gb) 3000Mhz

    • This ram is fine for most tasks. It has a good clock speed to handle your programs and 16 gigabytes helps perform well in games and programs. $90

  • GPU: AMD Radeon 580

    • This is a great graphics card for most modern games and performs well for the price. $195.99 (with mail in rebate of $20)

  • Case: Rosewill Tyrfing

    • This case is a good size at an ATX Mid Tower. It has space for cable management and a glass panel to see inside. Good case for the incredibly low price. $30

  • PSU: Rosewill 650w power supply

    • A decent semi-modular psu that can handle all components in this build. $60

  • Hard drive: Seagate 1tb hard drive

    • Relatively inexpensive hard drive. Has enough space for many programs and games to be installed. $45

    TOTAL: $662 (Tax not included)
